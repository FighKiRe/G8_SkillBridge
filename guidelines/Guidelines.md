# SkillBridge Design System

## Overview

SkillBridge is a modern SaaS platform connecting fresh graduates and NEET youth with employers through real-world micro-projects. The design system emphasizes clarity, professionalism, and accessibility while maintaining a youth-friendly approachability.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Primary Blue**: `#2563EB` - Main interactive elements, CTAs
- **Green Accent**: `#10B981` - Success states, achievements
- **Success Color**: `#22C55E` - Positive feedback, completion states

### Neutral Colors
- **Light Background**: `#F8FAFC` - Page backgrounds
- **Surface Cards**: `#FFFFFF` - Card backgrounds, elevated surfaces
- **Border Color**: `#E2E8F0` - Dividers, borders
- **Primary Text**: `#1E293B` - Headings, primary content
- **Secondary Text**: `#64748B` - Supporting text, labels

## Typography

- **Headings**: Poppins SemiBold (600)
- **Body**: Inter Regular (400)
- **UI Elements**: Inter Medium (500) for buttons and labels

## Components

Use consistent reusable components: buttons, cards, forms, navigation, badges, tags, analytics widgets.
